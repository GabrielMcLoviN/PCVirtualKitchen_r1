Movement Controls

Mobile & touch devices: Move around the kitchen by touching anywhere on the screen and dragging your finger across it.
Mouse & keyboard: Move around the kitchen by clicking your mouse cursor, holding it down as you drag across the screen.

Navigation

Arrows will take you to a different part of the kitchen.
Pampered Chef icons will take you to the info panel of the product closest to it.
